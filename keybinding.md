# miniRT Keybindings

## FOV
Decrease FOV: `[` <br>
Increase FOV: `]` <br>

## Object and Lights
Shift focus among objects and lights: `Tab` <br>

### Common

Move along x-axis: `←` `→` <br>
Move along y-axis: `↑` `↓` <br>
Move along z-axis: `i` `o` <br>

### Danger Zone
Increase light intensity: `PageUp` <br>
Decrease light intensity: `PageDown`
